  <p>
    Kitty Gifts ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application that lets you log your cat's hunting activity, compete with friends, and generate humorous eulogies for your cat's victims. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.
  </p>
  <p>
    By using Kitty Gifts, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.
  </p>

  <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>
  <p>We collect the following information when you use Kitty Gifts:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Google Account information</strong> — When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo URL from Google. We use this to identify your account.</li>
    <li><strong>Cat name and cat photo</strong> — You provide your cat's name during onboarding. You may optionally upload a photo of your cat. Cat photos are stored in your account and displayed within the app.</li>
    <li><strong>Kill log entries</strong> — Each time you log a kill, we store the animal type, the date, and the AI-generated eulogy associated with that entry.</li>
    <li><strong>Points and kill count</strong> — We maintain a running tally of your total kills and points to power the leaderboard feature.</li>
    <li><strong>Friend connections</strong> — If you use the invite feature, we store the user IDs of friends you have connected with in order to populate the shared leaderboard.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We do not collect your precise location, payment information, contacts, or any other sensitive personal data.</p>

  <h2>2. How We Use Your Information</h2>
  <p>We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve Kitty Gifts:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>To authenticate you and maintain your account across devices</li>
    <li>To display your cat's profile, kill log, and statistics within the app</li>
    <li>To power the Treat Leaderboard and show rankings among connected friends</li>
    <li>To generate AI-written eulogies for each kill entry via the Anthropic API</li>
    <li>To enable the friend invite system</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.</p>

  <p>Kitty Gifts uses the following third-party services to operate:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>Firebase (Google)</strong> — We use Firebase Authentication for sign-in and Firestore to store your account data, kill logs, and friend connections. Your data is stored on Google's servers subject to the
      <a href="https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Firebase Privacy Policy</a>.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Google Sign-In</strong> — Authentication is handled by Google. Your use of Google Sign-In is subject to
      <a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google's Privacy Policy</a>.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Anthropic API</strong> — When you log a kill, we send the animal type and your cat's name to Anthropic's API to generate a eulogy. No other personal information is sent to Anthropic. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by the
      <a href="https://www.anthropic.com/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Anthropic Privacy Policy</a>.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <p>
    Your data is stored in Google Firebase's cloud infrastructure. We use Firebase's built-in security rules to ensure that each user can only read and write their own data. Friend leaderboard data (cat name, kill count, points, and cat photo) is readable by connected friends within the app.
  </p>
